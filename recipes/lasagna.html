Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until tender, then set aside.
  2. In a pan, cook ground beef with chopped onion and garlic in olive oil until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper, and let it simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Repeat the layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for 30–40 minutes.
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
