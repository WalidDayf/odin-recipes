Pancakes

Pancakes are a breakfast favorite light, fluffy, and simple to make with basic ingredients. They’re perfect for weekend mornings or anytime you’re craving a quick treat. Top them with maple syrup, fruit, whipped cream, or even chocolate chips for an extra special meal.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter (melted)
  • Vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, stirring gently to form a smooth batter.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour small amounts of batter onto the pan to form pancakes.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
