Chicken Stir Fry Recipe

Chicken stir fry is a fast, flavorful dish made with tender chicken and fresh vegetables sautéed in a savory sauce. It’s a great weeknight meal that comes together quickly and can be served over rice or noodles. This dish is perfect when you want something healthy, colorful, and full of taste without spending too much time in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Bell peppers
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Cornstarch
  • Olive oil
  • Cooked rice (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice the chicken into thin strips and chop all vegetables.
  2. Make the stir fry sauce by mixing soy sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, and cornstarch.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok, and cook the chicken until golden and cooked through.
  4. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for about 5–7 minutes, until tender-crisp.
  5. Pour in the sauce and stir until everything is well coated and heated through.
  6. Serve hot on its own or over cooked rice.
